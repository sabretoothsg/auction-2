About This Note

The DAI CV 2023 Note is a fixed-term, fixed-rate debt instrument issued by MakerDAO. This note represents a claim on future DAI stablecoin yields generated through the Maker protocol's surplus buffer.

Investors in this note benefit from the stability of DAI, which is pegged to the US Dollar, while earning a competitive yield that is significantly higher than traditional savings accounts. The note matures on December 31, 2023, at which point the principal plus interest will be returned to the note holder.

This note is part of MakerDAO's strategy to diversify its treasury management and provide stable yield opportunities to the DeFi ecosystem.
