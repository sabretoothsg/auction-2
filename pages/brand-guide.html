Exchequer Finance Brand Guide

This page showcases the brand elements and components used throughout the Exchequer Finance platform.

Logo

Our primary logo is bold and confident. The concept of the checkered board is an instant link to our business model and core values.

The logo marque can be used as a stand-alone symbol where appropriate. The safe zones around the logo marque are determined by 50% of the height of the logo marque itself.

Graphic Device

Our graphic device is a direct extension of our logo. The checkered board along with the yellow square can be used as a flat pattern or skewed in space to fit the need of the application.

The checkered world of Exchequer Finance is inhabited by various different players, each represented by a different figurine.

Typography

Our word mark and headline typeface is Raleway, an elegant sans-serif typeface family. Its straight lines, minimal variation in line width give it a simple and clean look with some added unique character.

Heading 1 - Raleway Medium

Used for main page titles and hero sections.

Heading 2 - Public Sans Semibold

Used for section titles and important headings.

Heading 3 - Raleway Medium

Used for subsection titles and card headings.

Heading 4 - Raleway Medium

Used for smaller headings and important labels.

Our copy text typeface is Public Sans, a strong, neutral typeface designed for interfaces, text, and headings.

Body Text - Public Sans Regular

The main body text uses Public Sans font family. It's clean, modern, and highly readable even at smaller sizes. The default line height is 1.6 for optimal readability.

Light Text - Public Sans Light

Used for secondary information and subtle text elements.

This is a quote using Public Sans Italic. Quotes are styled with a left border and italic text.

Lists - Public Sans Regular

  • This is a bulleted list item
  • Lists use Public Sans Regular
  • They maintain the same line height as paragraph text

Caption - Public Sans Medium

Captions use Public Sans Medium at a smaller font size and dimmed color.
