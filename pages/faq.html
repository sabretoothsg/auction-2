Frequently Asked Questions

What is Exchequer Finance? +

Exchequer Finance is a decentralized fixed income protocol that enables users to access predictable yields through note trading. The protocol uses an innovative auction mechanism to ensure fair price discovery and optimal market efficiency.

How do auctions work? +

Auctions in Exchequer Finance use a Dutch auction mechanism where the price starts high and gradually decreases until all tokens are sold or the reserve price is reached. Each auction runs in rounds, typically 24 hours each.

Key auction features:
  • Multiple rounds with decreasing prices
  • Fair price discovery through market demand
  • Minimum bid size requirements
  • Maximum discount caps
